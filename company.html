Horizon was built by the dedicated folks at RethinkDB as well as thousands of contributors around the world. RethinkDB is an open-source database for the realtime web, and the foundation for Horizon. We beleive that "database tools should be indistinguishable from magic", and that goes for front end tools, too! The team (along with Skyla the dog) resides in Mountain View, California. Drop us a line if you'd like to stop by for lunch! info@rethinkdb.com
